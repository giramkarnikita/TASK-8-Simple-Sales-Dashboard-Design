
Simple Sales Dashboard - Insights
=================================

1. The Technology category had the highest total sales among all product categories.
2. The West region showed the highest sales performance, especially in Q3.
3. Sales showed an increasing trend from January to November, with peaks around mid-year.
4. Furniture category sales were stronger in the Central region compared to others.

These insights were derived using line, bar, and donut charts with slicers for Region and Category.
